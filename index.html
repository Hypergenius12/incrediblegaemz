About Incredible Gaemz

Developed and designed by Caleb Abbe

The Mission

I want to encourage families and friends across the country to spend more time with each other and less time staring at a screen. My company makes unique board and card games filled with entertainment for the whole family. This company plans to support the slowly dying industry of board and card games by giving people new fun options. This company is just starting up and needs all the support it can get. It is currently being run by a 16 year old junior in high school who is also enrolled and taking classes in Finger Lakes Community College.
